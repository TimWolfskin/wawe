About the School, Surfing, and the Canaries

The Canary Islands are not coincidentally called the European Hawai'i – it's the perfect place for surfing enthusiasts: consistent warm winds year-round, sandy beaches, kilometers of untouched expanses, and clear waters. And the true mecca for surfers becomes Fuerteventura – the second largest island in the archipelago.

Right here is the best place in Spain to receive surfing lessons. Moreover, aside from the massive Atlantic waves that come here in winter and autumn, Fuerteventura can also boast numerous shallow calm lagoons, perfectly suitable for training novice surfers.

Surfing in the Canary Islands is available year-round – both waves and suitable weather are always present. Moreover, both true professionals and beginners can find waves suitable for their level. It's not a coincidence that this place is considered the best for learning, and progressing to a new level here is possible without difficulties

Listing the advantages, it's also worth mentioning that Fuerteventura has the longest beaches in the Canaries. As a result, it's hard to imagine them crowded with tourists. You will encounter only those who, like you, share the same goal – learning to surf

Whether you're just looking to understand what surfing is all about or you've already experienced the thrill of riding waves and want to elevate your skills, our surfing school can offer training for all levels of experience.

Four hours of engaging lessons per day will help you confidently conquer the waves and make you feel like a true surfer

The main advantages of our Russian school include high-quality teaching and organization of lessons, safety (all our clients are insured with Allianz), excellent equipment and gear (provided for the entire course of training), including soft and safe boards for beginners.

First, we assess your riding level and physical fitness, and only then do we create a training program. And we can assure you that after the training is completed, you will not only gain a lot of wonderful experiences but also elevate your skill to a new level.

The instructors at our surfing school are among the best surfers in the Canary Islands. All members of our teaching team are certified members of the Canary Islands Surfing Federation. Some of them speak Russian, which certainly positions us as a Russian surfing school abroad!

The entire coaching staff regularly participates in seminars and professional development courses held worldwide by various renowned surf clubs and federations. Therefore, in our surfing school, you can rest assured that you're being taught by the best coaches!

The surfing training methodology is designed for beginners, intermediate level surfers, as well as top-level surfers.
